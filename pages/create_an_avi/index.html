Fantasy Avatar Building Help

We know that many different roleplay’s allow for different looking avatars to fit the roles. If you are playing a fantasy race on the sim, we ask that you keep in mind that we are looking to keep as normal and realistic appearances as we can. Meaning that skin tones should remain natural, however when changed there can be a few differences. Below we have compiled a list of places that carry items that we felt were in the realm of how we are hoping to keep the reality of the fantasy looking on the sim.
